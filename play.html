What do I do for fun?

Games

I love games, Neverwinter in particular! I also enjoy the Sonic the Hedgehog franchise.

Games in general give me the sense that I'm away from reality. They may give me some stress sometimes, but it's more casually stressful, if that makes sense. There's pressure, but not real pressure.

You can browse my game collection on Steam.

Music

Music is a huge part of my life. I listen to music when I'm doing almost everything, from playing games, to doing homework, to working on a project.

I love many different genres of music, but a few of my favorites are EDM, Rock, and Country.

You can check out what I've been listening to on my Spotify.

Writing

A hobby I haven't touched in a while, but a hobby nonetheless. I enjoy writing fictional stories and occasionally, poems.

I used to post my creations on Tumblr, but now I mostly reblog pictures of kittens. Not that you can ever have enough of those, or anything.
